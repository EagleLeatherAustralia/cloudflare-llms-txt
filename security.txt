# Notes for researchers
# - Please report vulnerabilities directly to the contact above before any
#   public disclosure. We will acknowledge within 5 business days.
# - We do not currently run a paid bug bounty. Good-faith reports are welcomed
#   and credited at the reporter's discretion.
# - Out of scope: rate-limit / DoS testing on production, any automated
#   scanning that materially impacts availability, social engineering of staff.
# - In scope: anything affecting customer data, payment integrity, account
#   takeover, or unauthenticated access to admin / order data.
